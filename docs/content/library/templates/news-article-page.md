# News Article Page

**Article Page:** The Article Page template, inherited from the base "Page" template in Sitecore, is designed to present detailed, narrative content such as news articles or blog posts.

This template features a header with a title, followed by a main content area for rich text and images, and a secondary column for related content or images.

*   **Structure**: Includes a title header, main content area for text and images, and a secondary column for related visuals.
    
*   **Use Case**: Suitable for news articles or blog posts with accompanying images.
